Hi there! I am a junior Electrical Engineering undergraduate at The Ohio State University passionate about developing innovative hardware that bridges technology and human impact. I am a hands-on, results-driven person and learn best through projects. My interests include PCB circuit design, medical devices, wearables. and RF systems. I’m motivated by challenging problems and take pride in finding solutions that not only work but excel.

One of my favorite memories is when I spoke at an end-of-season banquet and the swimmers I coached all summer collectively shouted, “That’s my favorite coach!” That moment reminded me why I love connecting with people and making a positive impact — something I strive to bring into my work as an engineer.

Outside of school and engineering, I like going out on hikes, running, rock climbing, photography, and football. I hope to someday move to the west coast so I can be near both bustling cities and beatiful mountains and nature.
